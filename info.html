Our Story



We're sure you've heard this one before ... but our wedding calls for a fresh reminder! On September 18, 2019, we first laid our eyes on each other, at the studio of a mutual friend who was hosting a sci-fi–themed LARP. Lacey ended up playing a healer, Alex a relationship expert… and the rest is history.

Locked down together during COVID, secretly, in a women-only collective housing, our love only grew stronger. And on July 18, 2023, Alex proposed, spontaneously while they were lounging in bed (although a lucky ruby ring had been burning a hole in his pocket for months, ever since he knew it belonged to Lacey).

A year into what would become our three-year engagement, we went on a mountain hiking trip with Lacey’s brother, Cooper. Our stay at the Hotel Regina in Mürren finally answered our year-long search for the perfect place to bring our families together.
Now, seven years into our love story, we are finally tying the knot, and we cannot wait to celebrate with ourloved ones.
