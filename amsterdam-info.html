Our Story


We’re sure you've heard this one before ... but our wedding calls for a fresh reminder! On September 18, 2019, we first laid our eyes on each other, at the studio of a mutual friend who was hosting a sci-fi–themed LARP. Lacey ended up playing a healer, Alex a relationship expert… and the rest is role playing history.

Locked down together during COVID, secretly, in a women-only collective housing, our love only grew stronger. And on July 18, 2023, Alex proposed, spontaneously while they were lounging in bed on a rainy afternoon, although a ruby ring had been burning a hole in his pocket for months).

Now, three years into our engagement, and seven into our love story, we are finally able to bring family and friends together for the first time. When we see each other on June 30th, we will just have gotten married in the court of Amsterdam (a small ceremony attended by our siblings) and we will continue celebrating this for the rest of the day with you. We cannot wait!
